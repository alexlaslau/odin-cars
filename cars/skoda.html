Skoda

Description

This is the car I currently own. It economical and practical, but it's a bit boring. You'll see why when you'll see the specs below.

The specs of the car.

  • 1.0 aspirated gas engine (MPI)
  • 5 speed manual
  • 75 HP

Even though I like the car and I enjoy driving it, the driving experience isn't as cool as it was with the Passat. The cool thing tho is that it's a bit newer, only 7 years old at the time of writing this. Some cool things I noticed about it:

  1. Driving gas cars is different than driving diesel cars. They seem to be a bit "cleaner" in the sense that the burning of the fuel seems to not be as dirty as the diesel burning.
  2. It's very economical, consuming between 4-6 liters for each 100km. Which is very nice.
